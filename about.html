about us

Rebels Sports Club

Reinvent oneself with cricket and Emphasize all could play together irrespective of their ethnicity, social status and background. Binding communities by Empowering Youth to Learn and lead along with Students from University of Regina

board members

Rebels Sports Club was founded by three students of university of regina. The club was founded with main motive to bring people from all races to unite by means of various sports activities, fight against discrimination, to give equal opportunties to everyone irrespective of their familiority with any sport.

26th Januvary, 2017 A new begining - Rebels

Rebels Sports Club was founded on 26th Jan 2017 by group of students from Univesity of Regina with main focus on taking sports to all communities and to bring people from different races together.

1st April, 2017 Rebuilding life for Refugee kids

Our voulenteering team along with University of Regina - Social Works Department conducted a program to help siriyan refugee kids rebuild their life through means of various sports activities. We had a very good session throughtout the summer helping lot of kids find their smiles back.

13th June, 2017 "Learn to Play Cricket Session"

As part of Rebels Social Work program we had the opportunity to introduce cricket to kids from Albert School, Regina. The kids learnt various aspects of cricket and were able to play a full game very quick. We are working toward making cricket more inclusive among different communities in Regina

24th August, 2017 Open Door Society - Regina

Rebels along with Open Door Society of Regina helping new immigrants to regina get a active session about various sporting activities that are avilable in Regina

8th October, 2017 Rebels Winter Leagues begins

Rebels Winter Cricket and batmitton leagues were introduced to keep the spirit of the game going. Inter club teams were formed who play against each other to win the prestigious Rebels Champions Title.
